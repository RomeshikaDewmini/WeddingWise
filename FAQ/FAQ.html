FAQ'S

How do I make a reservation for my wedding?

To make a reservation, simply visit our website and navigate to the "Reservations" page. Fill out the required details, such as the date, venue, and guest count, and submit the reservation request. Our team will then contact you to confirm the reservation and provide further assistance.

Can I visit the venue before making a reservation?

Yes, we highly recommend scheduling a visit to the venue before making a reservation. It will give you an opportunity to see the space, discuss specific requirements, and address any concerns you may have. Contact our team to schedule a visit or inquire about open house events.

What is the payment process for wedding reservations?

Upon confirmation of your reservation, we require a deposit to secure the date. The remaining balance can be paid in installments or according to the agreed-upon payment schedule. We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, bank transfers, or checks.

Can I customize the wedding package to suit my preferences?

Absolutely! We understand that every couple has unique preferences. Our team is dedicated to creating a personalized wedding experience for you. We offer various customizable packages that can be tailored to your specific needs, including menu options, decor choices, and additional services.

Is there a cancellation policy for wedding reservations?

Yes, we have a cancellation policy in place. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact our team for specific details regarding cancellations, refund eligibility, and any associated fees.
